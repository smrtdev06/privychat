⚠️  COMMON ISSUES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

❌ "Loading subscription options..." (stuck)
   → Product not created in Google Play Console yet
   → Create premium_yearly subscription

❌ "No subscriptions found"
   → Product ID typo (must be exactly: premium_yearly)
   → Base plan not activated
   → Wait 1-2 hours for propagation

❌ Products show "pending"
   → Wait 1-2 hours after creating/activating

❌ Can't find "Subscriptions" menu
   → Need to upload APK to Internal Testing first
   → Need Google Merchant Account setup
